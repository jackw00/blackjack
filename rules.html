Button Guide

  • Start Game/New Game: Clear current winnings or losses if applicable and start a new game, inputting a new name and starting amount of money.
  • Deal: Start a round of Blackjack, dealing you and the dealer one card each.
  • Hit: Ask the dealer for another card.
  • Stand: Tell the dealer you are done asking for more cards and let them play out their hand.

Note for this app:

  • There is only 1 player (you) and the dealer.
  • There is no real money - your chips and wagers are arbitrary numbers.
  • The generation of cards is based on a 52 card deck, which is shuffled (refilled) when the deck runs out of cards
  • Aces automatically and always count as 11.
  • Tens, Jacks, Queens, and Kings are all represented with the number 10.
